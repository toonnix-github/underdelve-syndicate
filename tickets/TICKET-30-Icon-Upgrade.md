# TICKET-30: Iconography Upgrade (V/R Badges)
**STATUS: BACKLOG**

## Description
The simple 'V' and 'R' circles on combat cards are too clinical.

## Requirements
- Replace 'V' with a Shield/Sword icon (Vanguard).
- Replace 'R' with a Bow/Eye icon (Rearguard).
- Use distinct colors (Amber/Steel vs Blue/Slate) to make them readable at a glance.
