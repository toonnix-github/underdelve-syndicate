# TICKET-19: Expedition Summary (The Run-Log)
**STATUS: BACKLOG**

## Description
Show the player their accomplishments at the end of the 30-minute run.

## Requirements
- Create a `SummaryView` component triggered by "Victory" or "Defeat".
- Display stats: Total Damage Dealt, Credits Found, Enemies Slain, and MVP hero.
- Allow the player to "Save Screenshot" or "Return to Menu".
