# TICKET-17: Altars of Ambition (Sacrifice Mechanics)
**STATUS: BACKLOG**

## Description
High-risk, high-reward interactables enhance the roguelite feel.

## Requirements
- Create an `ALTAR` interactable.
- When clicked, offer a "Deal": Spend 30% of current party HP for a permanent +5 ATK to one hero.
- Multiple types of altars (Power, Fortitude, Celerity).
