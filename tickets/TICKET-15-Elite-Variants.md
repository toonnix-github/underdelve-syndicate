# TICKET-15: Elite Champion Variants
**STATUS: BACKLOG**

## Description
Enemies feel too uniform. We should introduce "Elite" variants with unique prefixes and improved stats.

## Requirements
- Add a chance (e.g. 10%) for any generated enemy instance to be an "Elite".
- Elites get a +50% HP and +20% ATK boost.
- Elites get a visual indicator (e.g., a gold border or "The Cruel" prefix).
- Elites guarantee at least an Uncommon loot drop.
