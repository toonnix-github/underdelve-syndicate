# TICKET-21: Boss Room Presence (UI Visuals)
**STATUS: BACKLOG**

## Description
The Floor 3 Boss fight needs to feel different from a standard encounter.

## Requirements
- When the `activeEncounter` is a "Boss", the `BattlePhase` should use a different background (e.g., Throne Room).
- Add a large health bar at the top of the screen for the Boss.
- Play a "Shake" animation when the Boss executes their turn.
